# Blue Ox POS & Management System
## For Posh Nail Care

Welcome to your new salon management system! Here's a complete overview of what's available and ready to use.

---

## 🎯 What You Get

A modern, tablet-friendly platform designed specifically for salons. Manage your entire operation from one place: client records, services, sales transactions, staff, and loyalty rewards.

---

## ✅ Core Features (Fully Working)

### 📱 **Point of Sale (POS)**
- **Tablet-optimized interface** for quick checkout at the service station
- **Fast client lookup** – search by name or phone number
- **Service selection** – browse all available services organized by category
- **Shopping cart** with quantity controls
- **Multiple payment methods tracked at checkout:**
  - Cash
  - MTN Mobile Money
  - Airtel Money
- **Instant receipt generation** with unique receipt numbers
- **Automatic loyalty points** calculation based on purchase amount

### 👥 **Client Management**
- **Full client database** with contact information and visit history
- **Loyalty points tracking** – see who your best customers are
- **Lifetime value metrics** – total spent, total visits, last visit date
- **Birthday tracking** – stay connected with your clients
- **Quick client creation** – add new clients from the POS interface
- **Client profiles** – view complete service history and points balance

### 💇 **Services Management**
- **Online service catalog** with pricing for each service
- **Category organization** (e.g., Haircut, Shaving, Styling, Coloring, etc.)
- **Service descriptions** and duration tracking
- **Pricing flexibility** – update prices anytime
- **Service status control** – activate/deactivate services anytime

### 💰 **Sales & Transactions**
- **Complete sales history** – view every transaction ever processed
- **Real-time transaction dashboard** with filtering by:
  - Date range (Today, This Week, This Month, All Time)
  - Payment method (Cash, MTN Money, Airtel Money)
  - Client or receipt search
- **Sales analytics:**
  - Total daily/monthly sales
  - Transaction count
  - Average order value
  - Payment breakdown
- **Export to CSV** – download transaction reports for accounting
- **Transaction details modal** – click any transaction to see full details
- **Resend receipts** – notify customers via SMS if they missed the first one

### 🎁 **Loyalty Program**
- **Automatic points allocation** – customers earn points with every purchase
- **Loyalty member tracking** – see who qualifies for rewards
- **Tiered rewards** – set up different reward levels
- **Points balance display** – clients can see their points in real-time
- **Progress tracking** – monitor qualification toward reward tiers

### 📊 **Dashboard**
- **Sales overview** – today's sales, monthly revenue at a glance
- **Key metrics:**
  - Total revenue (today & month)
  - Transaction count
  - Active service count
  - Loyalty program members
  - Average order value
- **Recent visits snapshot** – quickly review latest transactions
- **Quick navigation** to POS, Sales, or any section

### 👔 **Staff Management**
- **Staff directory** with roles (Owner, Manager, Cashier, Stylist)
- **Performance metrics:**
  - Daily sales tracking
  - Weekly sales totals
  - Transaction count per staff member
  - Last login tracking
- **PIN & password login** for secure access
- **Status management** – activate/deactivate staff

### 📞 **Customer Notifications**
- **SMS receipts** – send transaction receipts to customer phone numbers
- **Customizable message templates** – tailor notifications to your brand and include dynamic info
- **Manual send/resend option** – contact customers anytime from transaction details
- **WhatsApp integration** *(Coming Soon)* – upgrade to WhatsApp Business API for richer messaging

### 🔐 **Security & Access**
- **Secure staff login** with PIN or password
- **Role-based access controls** for key management actions
- **Session management** with automatic logout
- **Transaction audit trail** – see who deleted what and when

## 🚀 How to Get Started

### First Login
1. Visit **poshnailcare.com** or your salon's unique link
2. Log in with your staff phone number and PIN
3. Start processing transactions

### Daily Workflow
1. **Morning:** Check dashboard for overnight summary
2. **Service hours:** Use POS to process client transactions
3. **Throughout day:** Monitor Sales page for real-time metrics
4. **End of day:** Check daily totals and export if needed for accounting

### Managing Your Salon
- **Add new services** from the Services page
- **Register new clients** from POS or Client Management
- **View/manage staff** from Staff page
- **Track loyalty members** from Loyalty Program page
